Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section, or by using the tracking number sent to your email.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with tags attached. Some items like electronics have specific return conditions.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within Accra takes 1-2 business days. Nationwide delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery options are available.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), credit/debit cards, and cash on delivery for orders within Accra.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship within Ghana only. We're working on expanding to other West African countries soon.
